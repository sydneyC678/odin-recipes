Lasagna

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Beef and sausage
  • Garlic
  • Lasagna pasta noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. : Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
  5. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9Ã—13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  6. Top the noodles with some of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  7. Cover with foil and bake. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan, and bake for another 15 minutes.
  8. Layer: sauce - noodles - cheese ; Repeat until satisfied
  9. Bake in oven for 1 hour
